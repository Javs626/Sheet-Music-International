Site administrators believe music on this website is in public domain or has been uploaded by owner of copyright for distribution. 


This site is monitored and music found to infringe copyright will be removed.  Sheet music International is a public repository that serves as a collection agency only. 


We assume no liability or responsibility for downloading or uploading files not in public domain in your country.  Please respect copyright laws in your country.  Individuals may be held liable for copyright infringements by copyright owners.


General Copyright Guidlines: 

USA.  Works published before 1923 are public domain.  Copyright lasts for life of composer +  70 years

Canada, Japan, Chine:  Copyright lasts for life of composer + 50 years.
